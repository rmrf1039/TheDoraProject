춘천집닭갈비막국수

春川家辣炒雞

The first restaurant, 집닭갈비막국수, is located in the West Gate of Seoul（서대문구）, about 3 minutes walking from Exit 1 or 2 of the subway in Xinchun Station. Their spicy fried chicken and rice cakes are extremely delicious... It is the treat that will make you want to take one bite after another. Furthermore, their chicken is also very chewy, and the restaurant is not far from 이화여자대학교（Ewha Womans University）. You can go to the nearby 이화여자대학교 shopping district（梨大商圈）to stroll along the streets after finish the meal.

TIPS: Remember to bring some stomach medicine because Korean food is almost spicy. You will probably feel uncomfortable.

자문위원

六三冷麵

The second recommended restaurant is 자문위원 which has many restaurant chains in Seoul, but the one I went to is near the APM mall in Dongdaemun（동대문구）. The location of the restaurant is in the basement, so you need to walk down after seeing the sign, which is extraordinary. The restaurant is very famous because it was interviewed by the TV media before. Especially in summer, travelers visiting South Korea should really come to the restaurant to taste the delicious cold noodles and barbecue. It is a great way to cool off the heat!

TIPS: Buy and add funds to a T-money card, you can use it to take the subway in South Korea.

북촌한옥마을（北村韓屋）

A Korean costume experience is highly recommended for visitors to South Korea! We recommend you to visit 북촌한마을 in the Samcheong-dong（삼청동）area of Seoul, where the culture of Korean houses has been preserved for 600 years. It is also a popular location for many Korean TV dramas and variety shows, making it a great place to take pictures anywhere. I really love it!

TIPS: Pre-plan the lines of subway in Seoul, South Korea.

TIPS: Shop around for the cheapest price because there are lots of the same commodities in different shops in Seoul.

서울타워（首爾塔）

A famous landmark located on the South Mountain in Seoul, which is one of the must-see tourist spots for people visiting Seoul. It is also a wonderful place for couples to visit, as it has a very beautiful collection of blessings, namely the Lovers'Lock. It symbolizes a couple from now on tightly locked together. That is too sweet!

덕수궁（德壽宮）

It is one of the five major palaces in South Korea, which is a world heritage site in Seoul, except for Jongmyo. After buying a ticket, you can enter the palace through the main gate of 덕수궁, which is the place shown in the picture. After entering, you will see the houses and palaces where the royal family used to live that makes you feel as if you have traveled back in time.

TIPS: Prepare to travel with a good mood.
